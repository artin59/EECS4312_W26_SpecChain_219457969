# Requirement ID: FR_auto_1
- Description: The system shall provide a clear and easily accessible subscription management page that allows users to view, update, and cancel their subscriptions at any time.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Fiona
- Traceability: Derived from review group A1
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a user is logged into their Calm account, When they navigate to the account settings, Then they can view their current subscription plan, see the next renewal date, and cancel their subscription with a single click.

# Requirement ID: FR_auto_2
- Description: The system shall display transparent and accurate pricing information, including all potential charges and fees, on the subscription sign-up page and throughout the app.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Fiona
- Traceability: Derived from review group A1
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a user is viewing the subscription options, When they select a plan, Then they can see a detailed breakdown of the costs, including any promotional discounts, taxes, and fees, before confirming their purchase.

# Requirement ID: FR_auto_3
- Description: The system shall automatically send a confirmation email with a clear summary of the subscription details, including the plan, price, and renewal date, after a user signs up for or changes their subscription plan.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Fiona
- Traceability: Derived from review group A1
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a user has made a change to their subscription, When the change is confirmed, Then they receive an email within 5 minutes that includes a summary of their new subscription details and a link to manage their subscription.
